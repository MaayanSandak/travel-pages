Budapest

Budapest, the capital of Hungary, is a city steeped in history and culture. Located on the Danube River, it offers a unique blend of thermal baths, stunning architecture, and vibrant nightlife. Visitors can explore the city's many museums, galleries, and historical landmarks, making it a must-visit destination for any traveler.
